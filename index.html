Single-file workshop tool

Team Protection & Escalation Builder

A calm, practical workshop space for defining when concerns stay in team dialogue, when protected consultation is needed, and when formal or independent escalation should be activated.

Core framing

Escalation is not a sign of failure. It is a pre-agreed protection path when people, roles, or decisions come under pressure.

Use it in 45-90 minutes

Move through the seven steps, edit the default model, and leave with a one-page draft your team can review together.

Built for sensitive contexts

Especially useful for university teams, ethics committees, governance bodies, and groups where hierarchy affects whether people speak up.
